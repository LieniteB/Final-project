It all started simple

As everyone does, me and Liene set some new goals for 2022. We were both keen on changing out lives for the better, exercising, eating healthier, making new friends and having more fun. And the first thing we both decided to tackle first was our goal to read more. But just as one does, we would procrastinate and fail. So we thought... What could make us motivate to read more? What would be an easy way to make reading a new exciting habit for us? And thus, with just a single New Year's resolution a new idea was born.

AN EASY WAY FOR A NEW HABIT
